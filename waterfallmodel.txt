Advantages
1)Rigidity and inflexibility
2)Limited customer feedback
3)Clear structure and progression
4)Documented process with comprehensive records
5)Easy project management
6)Limited client involvement throughout
7)Predictable timelines


Disadvantages:

1)Rigidity and inflexibility
2)Limited customer feedback
3)High risk of project failure if requirements change
4)Late detection of defects
5)Not suitable for large projects
6)Dependency on initial specifications
7)Long delivery time
